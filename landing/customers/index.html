Three design partner slots.

PQSafe AgentPay is building toward a small group of named institutional design partners through 2026. These are not customers in the conventional sense — they ship alongside us, get direct co-design input on AP2-PQ, and trade a 12-month dedicated engineering relationship for a logo placement and reference call.

Slot 1
Reserved for a regulated financial institution
HKMA / MAS / FCA / FFIEC-regulated. AI agent payments or treasury automation.
Slot 2
Reserved for a payment / fintech infrastructure operator
Card issuer, virtual card, account-to-account, or stablecoin rail.
Slot 3
Reserved for an AI agent platform / framework
Team shipping agents that move money to production.

What we ship together

  • Dedicated integration engineering. Direct line to the co-founders. We help your team wire PQSafe into your existing payment flow, agent runtime, or compliance stack — not just docs and Discord.
  • Co-designed mandate schema. If your use case needs extra mandate fields (e.g., regulatory reference number, treasury account class, intent hash), we add them to the AP2-PQ profile rather than asking you to fork.
  • Conformance test vectors specific to your rail. We extend the public test vector set so your edge cases become part of the canonical spec.
  • Joint white paper (optional). One-pager or full doc co-authored with your team, published on both sites and submitted to the relevant standards body (FIDO TWG, IETF) as evidence of production deployment.
  • Pre-audit findings sharing. Our internal security review log and the eventual Trail of Bits report are shared under NDA before broader publication.

What we ask for

  • A named integration project with a 12-month timeline and an internal exec sponsor at your organization.
  • Permission to publish your logo on this page and in investor materials once the integration ships to your production environment.
  • One 30-minute reference call per quarter for our investor due-diligence and prospective design partner conversations.
  • Honest feedback in writing. A short quarterly note — what works, what doesn't, what we should ship next — published on the joint white paper if there is one.

What we do not ask for

  • No fee for the design partner relationship itself. The infrastructure (signing, ML-DSA-65 verification, SDKs, conformance, RFC contributions) is and remains free under Apache-2.0.
  • No exclusivity. You can use any complementary stack (Stripe Issuing for cards, Airwallex for FX, Plaid for ACH, etc.). PQSafe wraps the authorization layer; it does not replace the rails.
  • No equity. This is a technical partnership, not a strategic investment relationship.
  • No press obligation beyond logo placement. You opt into co-marketing where it suits you.

Timeline

  1. Today – June 30, 2026: selecting design partners for the three slots above. Target close on Slot 1 by August 1, 2026 (signed LOI).
  2. July 2026 – February 2027: integration engineering, conformance test vector extensions, internal pre-audit findings exchange.
  3. Q1 2027: joint white paper and production launch announcement; logos go live on this page; first reference calls available.
  4. Q2 2027: follow-on cohort for paid customers (pricing per main site: US$29/mo + 0.3% above $10K SUM).

How to apply

Email raymond@pqsafe.xyz with:

  • Your organization, your role, and the named exec sponsor.
  • One paragraph on the integration project — what you'd like PQSafe to gate, what success looks like.
  • Whether your timeline aligns with a Q3 2026 integration start.

First reply within 48 hours. If we are not the right fit, we will tell you in the same reply.
